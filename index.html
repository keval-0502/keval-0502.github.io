Hi, I am Keval Bavisi.

Greetings, I am Keval Bavisi and i am a graduate student in MS Cybersecurity course at Northeastern University in Boston. In my professional career, I successfully collaborated with SIEMENS on a project funded by the Indian Government for the Vulnerability Assessment of Smart Meters and AMI Infrastructure. I am actively seeking a summer co-op opportunity in Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics.
